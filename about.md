# About These Workshops

The SUNY Poly AIX Cameroon Workshops are a free capacity-building series for graduate researchers and faculty at partner universities in Cameroon.

## Who We Are

**SUNY Polytechnic Institute** is a public technology-focused university in Utica, NY. The AIX Center (Artificial Intelligence Exploration Center) develops AI literacy programs for researchers, educators, and professionals.

## Partner Universities

**University of Buea** and **University of Yaoundé I** are collaborating with SUNY Poly to bring practical AI research tools to graduate students and faculty in science and engineering.

## What We Teach

Every session uses free, low-bandwidth-friendly tools and draws examples directly from participants' own research fields. No prior AI experience required.
