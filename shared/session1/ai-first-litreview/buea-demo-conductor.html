2 Discover the literature Perplexity

Paste the research question into Perplexity. It returns a structured framework and sources with inline citations.

Pre-generated output · 1-perplexity.md

Review focus

Three linked questions: where to interconnect, how to operate safely, how to justify economically. Multicriteria approach for busbar selection.

Technical themes

Power-flow behavior, voltage stability, frequency deviation, transient performance, contingency analysis, line-loss sensitivity.

Economic themes

Capital cost, reinforcement cost, cost of losses, avoided generation cost. Regional studies show 6–20% cost savings.

Strategic themes

Energy security, regional integration, resilience, decarbonization. "No-regrets" resource sharing.

AI methods

Load forecasting, busbar ranking, loss prediction, voltage/frequency assessment — neural networks, genetic algorithms, particle swarm optimization.

Research gap

Many studies evaluate interconnection benefits regionally; fewer translate them into a busbar-selection method for a specific cross-border corridor.

4 Read the sources Gemini

Upload the three PDFs plus the Perplexity output to Gemini. Gemini's large context window reads all three papers together.

Pre-generated output · 3-gemini.md

Overlaps

All three sources converge on: renewable integration, economic optimization, and strategic siting as foundational themes.

Distinctions

Kelly et al. = micro/local (HOMER). Wu et al. = macro/regional (MapRE/GIS). Imdadullah et al. = global hardware review.

Gaps

Geographic gap (Central Africa omitted). Technical busbar gap (no corridor-level analysis). AI methodological gap (none of the three use AI optimization).

"A critical gap remains in combining these dimensions using AI to select the optimal busbar for the Cameroon-Chad corridor."

5 Interrogate the sources NotebookLM
What NotebookLM does

Document-grounded only

You upload your PDFs. The model is constrained to those sources — it cannot draw from its training data. Every answer is cited to a specific passage. It cannot confabulate.

Why it matters here

Gemini read everything together and mapped the landscape. NotebookLM lets you verify specific claims against specific text. Ask it something Gemini said and confirm whether the papers actually support it.

Audio overview

NotebookLM can generate a podcast-style conversation summarizing your sources — useful for orientation and for students who absorb material by listening.

The key feature

Most AI tools expand outward — connecting your question to everything they know. NotebookLM contracts inward — staying inside the literature you gave it. That constraint is the point.
